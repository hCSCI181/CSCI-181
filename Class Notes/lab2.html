Black Bear Bakery and Eatery

Who We Are

The Black Bear Bakery and Eatery is a family-owned and one-of-a-kind restaurant. Whenever possible, our dishes and treats are made with locally sourced ingredients. We are conveniently located in downtown Whitefish, Montana.

Our Menu

The menu at Black Bear Bakery and Eatery is constantly changing with the seasons, talents, and moods of our chefs and bakers. To find out what we are serving each week, visit our Facebook page or drop by the restaurant.

  • Blueberry and cranberry poppyseed muffins
  • Goats milk oatmeal made with Montana-made oats
  • Locally sourced cheeses, meat, and veggies!
